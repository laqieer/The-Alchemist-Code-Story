Day of Strategy Implementation
Wadatsumi - Unspecified Port

Zain		 We arrived. It's the Wadatsumi port.

Kagura		 You're right. Finally.

Zain		 Kanon and the other's support troops are scheduled to land from separate locations.

Zain		 A rendezvous point has been set up, so first let's head there.

Kagura		 Um, I have a question... Why didn't we leave together?

Zain		 First, it was to prevent our movements from being picked up by Orion from Greed Dike, who is eyeing the Mikaboshi.

Zain		 Also, to avoid the eyes of a portion of the Holy Order who has an eerie attachment to Sacred Stones.

Kagura		 The Holy Order...is completely a part of us.

Zain		 This may be unwarranted, but...just in case. They're in a much better situation to get information than Greed Dike.

Zain		 In fact, right before departure, they congratulated me. Depending on the way you look at it, they could be scrounging for information.

Zain		 Anyway, it's best to get close to them before they find out about the Mikaboshi.

Kagura		 That's why we made them move separately from us.

Zain		 Yes.

Zain		 In order to make it look we're going to multiple locations, we had the gathered Paladins separate into a few groups and head in different directions.

Zain		 They're all going their individual routes but will meet up in the same spot. From there, the plan begins.

Kagura		 I see...So you're being extra careful.

Zain		 It can't be helped.

Zain		 It's always best to avoid things that may turn into problems down the road.

Kagura		 Understood. Then all that's left is for us to head to that rendezvous point!

Kagura		 Even as we speak, look what's decided to show up!

Zain		 This area used to be inhabited. Wild monsters, huh? Let's quickly take them out!
