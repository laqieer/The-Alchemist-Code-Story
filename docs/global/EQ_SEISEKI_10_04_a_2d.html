Northern Pride. Headquarters of the Holy Guard.

Suzuka		 Mistress!

Kanon		 I recalled that I'd heard of the Vessel of Reincarnation before.

Kazahaya		 What is that you're holding?

Kanon		 It's a document that Lord Commander Zain entrust to me.

Kanon		 There is unique information in here regarding the station of a Lord Commander and other information that must not be released to the public.

Kazahaya		 The Lord Commander's notes!

Suzuka		 Does it mention the Vessel of Reincarnation?

Kanon		 Yes. The Vessel of Reincarnation concerns Nimul and Emmel.

Kazahaya		 The legendary siblings who created the Tower of Babel...?

Kanon		 Yes. According to history, they were the founders of Alchemy...said to be closest to god.

Kanon		 It's not public knowledge, but it has been said that every few hundreds of years their souls are reincarnated, thus returning to this world.

Suzuka		 Returning to this world...?!

Kazahaya		 The founders of Alchemy...?!

Kanon		 Yes. At least according to what Lord Commander Zain wrote here.

Kanon		 Apparently, Nimul and Emmel will descend in a Vessel that contains a Sacred Stone within their body.

Suzuka		 In that case...Lion Monarch Orion really is a Vessel of Reincarnation?!

Kanon		 At least he fits the criteria...that is if the legend is true.

Kazahaya		 Well, uh...what exactly happens when that reincarnation takes place.

Kanon		 If the reincarnation occurs, the Tower of Babel will ascend to the heavens... They will transcend the gods...eventually bringing about the end of this world.

Kanon		 That's what's written here. I don't know anything beyond that.

Kazahaya		 The...end of this world...?!

Suzuka		 The Tower of Babel will ascend to the heavens...

Suzuka		 That's referring to that Babel Ruins in the center of Northern Pride, right?

Kazahaya		 I've heard that those ruins are the decayed form of the former Tower of Babel.

Kanon		 That's why Orion's ultimate destination is Northern Pride.

Kanon		 He wants to gather all the Sacred Stones throughout the world in order destroy its equilibrium. Then, he plans to make his long-awaited march here.

Kazahaya		 I-in other words...if he reaches Greed Dike...

Suzuka		 This world will end...?!

Kanon		 No...there's no guarantee that's the case. Think about it...their reincarnation only occurs once every few hundred years.

Kanon		 If it was a certain thing, this world would've have been destroyed long ago.

Kazahaya		 Right...but...what does it mean?

Kanon		 According to the secret history written in this document...

Kanon		 When Nimul's Vessel of Reincarnation appears, it has been said that Emmel's Vessel will appear at the same time.

Suzuka		 Emmel as well?

Kanon		 Yes. Both Nimul and Emmel...

Kanon		 One of the Alchemists will wield the Alchemy of Might and the other will wield the Alchemy of Compassion.

Kanon		 The two forces will be like water and oil. They will slay each other and things will continue as they have always been.

Suzuka		 So Emmel will stop Nimul then?

Kazahaya		 That means...

Kazahaya		 There is a possibility that there is another individual in this world besides the Lion Monarch Orion who possesses a Sacred Stone within their body!

Suzuka		 You're right...!

Suzuka		 The countries where the location of the Sacred Stone have not been made clear are Wratharis...and Slothstein...

Kanon		 Even if we were to search both countries for the Vessel of Reincarnation...

Kanon		 I doubt we would find out anything in time for Greed Dike's assault, but...

Kanon		 Kazahaya, just in case, send envoys to both countries.

Kazahaya		 Understood!

Kanon		 I will report all of this to the Emperor. Suzuka, I'll leave matters here to you.

Suzuka		 Got it!

Kanon		 The Lion Monarch Orion seeks to destroy the equilibrium of power in this world and, by doing so, obtain Nimul's power.

Kanon		 It seems like this idea of his is more than a pipedream!
