Deneb		 Well...

Albea		 You're right. I might as well have had those men take me with them.

Deneb		 Albea!

Albea		 Heh. I'm joking. Don't worry. I won't really go to see him. Right now, I can't.

Deneb		 ... ...

Albea		 When we were children...before I became aware of things, my parents split up. I went with my father and my older brother went with my mother.

Albea		 I don't really remember my mother's face, but I remember meeting her just once.

Albea		 At that time, she said to me, "You have a brother who is aiming to become an excellent Alchemist.

Albea		 I'm sure he will become a great Paladin and a wonderful Alchemist. He will be a brother that will make you proud."

Deneb		 I see.

Albea		 Until then, I didn't even know I had a brother.

Albea		 But, those words stuck with me for some reason. That may be why...

Albea		 I wanted to become a sister that my brother could be proud of. Although I wasn't that great, I did my best to improve as a mechanic.

Albea		 I wanted to become an excellent artisan and take over my father's studio...so that my brother would have a place to come home to.

Albea		 I wanted a place that my mother could come home to as well.

Albea		 But...I couldn't do it in time and she died.

Albea		 I wanted to create and protect a home where he could always come back to. I wanted to make my brother proud.

Deneb		 I see. So that was your connection to your brother.

Albea		 Yes. But my brother quit the Holy Guard and gave up on being an Alchemist.

Albea		 I want to meet him. I want to ask why he gave up on being an Alchemist, but...

Albea		 I don't know what I'm going to do with that information once I find out.

Deneb		 You're scared of meeting your brother.

Albea		 You might be right.

Albea		 Sigh... When you gaze at me like that, I feel like talking forever.

Deneb		 You don't have to pay me anything. Instead...

Albea		 ...?

Albea		 Huh? What a hassle...

Deneb		 Come now. Please?

Deneb		 They're all things that I've received as gifts for reading fortunes. They're broken, so I can't use them, but I don't have the heart to throw them away.

Albea		 Sigh... So? Do you want me to pack them in a box?

Deneb		 Yes. Please!

Albea		 Hey...this right here.

Albea		 This one as well. You just need to change the spring and it'll be okay.

Albea		 As for this one...it seems like the gear is rusty, but, if you brush and polish it, it'll still move.

Deneb		 You really do know your way around these things, don't you!

Albea		 Of course, I do. I'm an artisan.

Albea		 As for this, you can't use it anymore, but you can use the parts for this one.

Albea		 You're right.

Albea		 I'm sure there will come a time when I'll find answers about my brother as well, right?

Deneb		 Yes. I'm sure of it.

Albea		 I'll do my best so that, when I meet him someday, he can be the one that says, "I'll be an older brother that my sister can be proud of."

Deneb		 May you be guided by beautiful stars. Be well, Albea.

Albea		 You too.
