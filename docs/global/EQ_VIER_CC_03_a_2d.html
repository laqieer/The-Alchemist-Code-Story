Letitia		 For eons... As countless ages passed... The souls of high elves, hunted by the other races, must have sowed the potential for retribution within our very DNA.

Letitia		 And so, the soul of a high elf slain by one of their own would curse their kin. After all, were high elves not the most innocent? The most pure?

Letitia		 Yet the sinners would be transformed by the blood, Alchemia, and souls of the high elves they killed, marking their bodies with the evil deed they had done. Even their descendants were cursed to carry that mark.

Letitia		 The sin of betraying their people carried forevermore. Inherited even by the descendants with no memories of committing such sin.

Letitia		 That is right. We dark elves are sacrifices. Our existence served to prove the ideology that the high elves are eternally pure and incorruptible.
Ephylinde That is why I left my home behind.

Letitia		 Trying to find yourself?
Ephylinde They spend their near-eternal lifespans in a small world, condemning other races, letting their egos swell to unbearable levels... I was suffocating there.
Ephylinde I was afraid of hating myself...of hating the high elf race... So I wanted to find my own way to exist as a high elf.

Letitia		 Did you enjoy your journey?
Ephylinde I traveled through many nations over a very long time, hoping for somewhere more comforting than home.
Ephylinde I visited many high elven communes around the world.

Letitia		 They needed that to preserve their integrity.

Letitia		 To protect their kin, they needed to separate themselves from others. To protect themselves, they needed to have easily identifiable enemies.

Letitia		 There is no need to blame yourself.
Ephylinde Better to attack myself than your people.

Letitia		 Oh, did you not notice?
