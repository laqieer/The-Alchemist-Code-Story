Eizan		 Hmm... I still have a long way to go.

Rosa		 Do you have a minute?

Eizan		 What is it?

Rosa		 I heard that I could find the "ultimate blacksmith" around here.

Rosa		 From your appearance...would that be you?

Eizan		 I'm the "ultimate blacksmith"? Did you believe the rumors? Are you an idiot?

Rosa		 I see. I guess I was mistaken.

Rosa		 That being said, I'll be able to tell by examining the weapon you're holding. Can you lend it to me for a second?

Eizan		 Hmph. Do what you want.

Rosa		 It looks like an ordinary sword...but...there are overlapping layers of iron. On top of that, it's skillfully built.

Rosa		 Hmm. Not bad. Are you going to try and get away with just showing me this?

Eizan		 I make no claim to being the "ultimate blacksmith."

Eizan		 Besides, what you're holding in your hand is far from anything that can be defined as "ultimate."

Rosa		 What do you mean? It doesn't seem that badly built.

Eizan		 It's true that it cuts well. However, it lacks any semblance of a soul that latches onto its wielder.

Eizan		 Do you know what I'm talking about?

Rosa		 A soul... Indeed, a soul...heheh. The primary emotion of destruction dwells within a weapon. I guess you could call that a soul.

Rosa		 Heheh. I like the way you think.

Rosa		 It isn't that this sword lacks a soul. It's still "dead." It hasn't been blessed with a worthy wielder yet.

Eizan		 Oh?

Rosa		 I'll awaken the soul of this sword. Watch.

Rosa		 ...?!

Rosa		 (Th-the sword...broke...)

Eizan		 ... ...

Rosa		 Well...um...about this...

Rosa		 Ah. Let's try to stay calm for now, shall we?

Eizan		 ...?!

Rosa		 He-hey...it's a hassle to dispose of defective weapons, right?

Rosa		 That's why I broke it for you. In other words, you're allowed to thank me, you know?

Eizan		 ...Hey.

Rosa		 I-it's the soul. This sword didn't have a soul.

Rosa		 Speaking of swords with souls, you're talking about enchanted ones that can kill gods and absorb the blood of demons, right? Try making something like that next...eh?

Eizan		 I see. In that case, hurry on home. I've got nothing like that here for you.

Rosa		 Oh! He-hey, wait.

Eizan		 What? Is there anything else you want?

Rosa		 Although it may be broken, not many humans could forge a sword like this. Who are you?

Eizan		 I am no one. Just a blacksmith.

Rosa		 Stop feigning ignorance. There's no way a run-of-the-mill blacksmith could forge a blade so sharp.

Eizan		 What would a little wench like you know about anything? Haven't you done enough damage by breaking my sword?

Rosa		 Are you trying to imply that my observation is flawed?

Eizan		 Isn't it? It's hard to believe anyone wielding a weapon in such flashy outfit has a good eye for anything.

Rosa		 My appearance has nothing to do with this! Also, don't make fun of my holy garments!

Eizan		 Holy garments...?

Rosa		 This garment has been dyed with the bloody tears of a fallen angel. This is the holy garment promised to me that I alone am allowed to wear.

Eizan		 What the...? And who promised them to you?

Rosa		 A god.

Eizan		 I see. You should get checked by a doctor as soon as possible. Good luck.

Rosa		 Wait. Don't you understand the sublimity of these holy garments?

Eizan		 No, nor do I want to. Both weapons and armor are tools to be used on the battlefield.

Eizan		 Only an idiot or someone with too much time on their hands would lavish so much time on a battle tool.

Rosa		 Wh-what did you say?!

Rosa		 I-I won't forgive you. You're the one who got all high-and-mighty about a soul!

Rosa		 How dare you make fun of me. I'll send you to the underworld where you can mull over what's happened here!
