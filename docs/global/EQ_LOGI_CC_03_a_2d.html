Logi		 ...! I...I don't know...what happened to Dias, but...Agatha...Agatha...!

Setsuna		 No... Did she...die?

Logi		 I'm not sure, but...she suddenly disappeared.

Setsuna		 Disappeared? What do you mean? Well, I guess you wouldn't know.

Setsuna		 But at least there's hope.

Logi		 Hope?

Setsuna		 If you haven't seen her die and you don't know where she is, then that means she's alive somewhere, right?

Logi		 ...! Ohh!

Logi		 That's right...I was blown away. Blown away? Did that happen to Agatha too?

Setsuna		 I'm sure that Agatha is alive. You couldn't protect her, right? Just make sure that you do next time.

Setsuna		 But, in your current state, you wouldn't be able to, Logi. You've forgotten the basics of being a knight.

Logi		 The basics of being a knight...?

Setsuna		 It's to not close your eyes. I taught you that, didn't I?

Setsuna		 You closed your eyes each time I hit you.

Logi		 I-I won't close them anymore!

Setsuna		 Even if your eyes are open, the eyes of your heart are closed.

Setsuna		 Logi...did you see the enemy? Who was it that brought about the downfall of Envylia?

Logi		 The Holy Guard. I was somewhat aware of this. But...I couldn't believe that the Holy Guard of Babel...those responsible for upholding justice within the continent...would do something like that.

Logi		 We just wanted peace with Wratharis...ack!

Setsuna		 So you were fighting for peace, Logi...but, deep down in your heart, you didn't want to wield your sword.

Setsuna		 You believe that obtaining from wielding your sword will bring about peace.

Logi		 I-isn't that the case? I wanted a world where we didn't have to fight...where we could understand each other!

Setsuna		 But your adversaries ruthlessly directed their blades towards you. Still, you didn't want to use your sword. Right?

Setsuna		 If you were to cut someone down or if someone had to be cut down, you prefer that it were you. Right?

Logi		 ...!

Setsuna		 I, too, have thought that way in the past. Still, that doesn't stop them from coming and taking everything you hold dear. After all...justice belongs to those who are victorious.

Logi		 Does justice belong to those who are...victorious?

Setsuna		 Right now, do you know what people are calling your father?

Setsuna		 The great sinner. That's the lie that the Holy Guard has been spreading.

Logi		 No...no! What did father ever do to be called that?! Why is the Holy Guard doing this?!

Setsuna		 History is written by the victors...and victory is only obtained by taking something from others.

Setsuna		 Logi...you don't want to take anything, you don't want to lose anything, and you don't want others to lose anything. You've been that way ever since you were small.

Logi		 Do you think that way of thinking is the source of my weakness?!

Setsuna		 No. Your way of thinking isn't wrong. But, in order to achieve this, you have no choice but to become strong.

Setsuna		 To not take, to not lose, and to not have others lose...

Setsuna		 Which is justice...? You need the power to choose your own justice.

Logi		 The power to choose...?

Setsuna		 Yes. You need to remember that Logi. So...come. I'll teach you.

Setsuna		 A skillful swordsman will see everything from the tip of his blade. The breathing, movement, and even the intentions of his opponent. Logi...it's about time you open your eyes.
