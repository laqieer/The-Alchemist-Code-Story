Courage		 Reagan, are you here? I need your help.

Reagan		 Incredible... I've never seen dissatisfaction levels on this wavelength before, let alone this high!

Courage		 Oh, shut up about your wavelengths. I need you to look at that gun you souped up for me. I think it's broken.

Reagan		 My weapons don't just 'break'. What in Babel have you been doing with it?

Courage		 What they usually pay us markswomen for. You know, scouting from up high, protecting VIPs...

Reagan		 Hmm...

Courage		 What? Got something to say?

Reagan		 Do you realize how delicate an instrument a long-range firearm is?

Reagan		 If you're going to keep using yours as a cudgel, you can find someone else to work on it.

Courage		 Was it really that obvious? I mean, I didn't have a choice! You should be thankful I made it back alive!

Courage		 Well, I guess it's not your fault. Honestly, if only those mercs didn't have such big mouths!

Courage		 This is all because they mentioned my dad...

Reagan		 Ah, yes, you did mention you were searching for your father. A famous mercenary, as I recall?

Courage		 That's right. I mean, he was actually just a sportsman, but he was still pretty famous in those circles.

Courage		 Anyway, I overheard a group of mercs talking about someone who sounded just like him.

Courage		 Only, they were saying he'd been seen visiting bandit hideouts and advising gang bosses... All sorts of bad rumors.

Reagan		 Isn't that good news? Now you might finally be able to track him down.

Courage		 I guess so, but I never thought he'd be working with criminals.

Reagan		 Is it really surprising that unsavory types would take an interest in him if he's famous in the mercenary world?

Courage		 They sometimes did in the past, but he always turned them down. I can't believe he'd change his mind now.

Reagan		 Hmm... So with that on your mind, you've been having trouble focusing on your work.

Courage		 ...

Reagan		 I'm confused. If you truly have faith in your father, your concerns are illogical.

Reagan		 Unless you're worried that maybe you don't believe in him as firmly as you thought.

Courage		 Of course I believe in him! I just...

Courage		 ...

Courage		 Maybe you're right. I've never really thought about why he left. I guess I was too scared to try.

Courage		 Perhaps we'd both be better off not seeing each other again.

Reagan		 You'll never know unless you confront him. If you can't face that, you should stop wasting valuable mental energy dwelling on him.

Reagan		 Otherwise you'll keep using your poor gun for things it wasn't meant for, and that wouldn't be fair to it. Speaking of which...

Courage		 Whoa, you fixed that quick! Hold on, where are you off to?

Reagan		 I've sent some prototypes to a certain village for field-testing. I want to see how they're doing.

Reagan		 Development and innovation requires action. If you'd rather waste your time here dwelling on useless things, you're free to do so.

Courage		 You know, you're being pretty insensitive to a girl in a crisis.

Reagan		 I'm just telling you the situation as I see it.

Courage		 You're really trying to get under my skin, aren't you? Fine, I get it, I'll go with you!
