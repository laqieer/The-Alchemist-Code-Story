Lisbeth		 Why...why do you go out of your way to die?!

Venus		 Because that is love, Lisbeth.

Lisbeth		 What...?

Venus		 The essence of love is self-sacrifice.

Venus		 Seeking the happiness of the one you love, without regards to what happens to yourself. That is true love.

Dias		 What an arrogant thing to say. Happiness cannot be bought with the life of a single individual.

Venus		 But what if it wasn't just one individual? I am the queen. I am loved by many vassals who have entrusted me with their love.

Venus		 The happiness of the queen is the future of the country. These countless lives will now become the cornerstone of this country.

Venus		 Would you call such a noble sacrifice an act of arrogance?

Dias		 It depends on the one who the sacrifice is made for. Sacrifices made for a twisted individual can lead to the destruction of a country!
