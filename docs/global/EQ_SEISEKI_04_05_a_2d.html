Kagura		 I've managed to complete inherit the Kagutsuchi. Does this mean training is complete?

Zain		 Yes. You did well. And I'm sorry.

Kagura		 Huh?

Zain		 Although the point was to push you to the edge, I sent out that one blow truly with the intention of killing you.

Zain		 You managed to accomplish this intensive training. I promise to repay you on the battlefield.

Kagura		 Oh, that? Don't worry about it. We were both giving it our all after all.

Zain		 I see. Once again, I'm sorry.

Zain		 That being said, your completed Kagutsuchi is truly stunning in its impact.

Zain		 It almost transformed into something beyond a Kagutsuchi.

Zain		 The legendary Kagutsuchi Kiraboshi that was in the literature must have been this technique.

Kagura		 Kagutsuchi Kiraboshi...

Zain		 Perhaps it is due to the aptitude of your Alchemia...as well as your feelings towards Kudan.

Kagura		 Ku-Kudan...?! Please, don't joke like that, Lord Zain!

Zain		 Hm? Did I hear you incorrectly? I thought I heard you say you want to rescue Kudan from the darkness.

Kagura		 We-well...

Zain		 Most likely, without knowing it, you had placed a lid on the large vessel of Alchemia within you.

Zain		 Perhaps it was simply due to fear of its enormous force or because you were afraid of controlling it.

Zain		 In either case, by clearing your heart, you were able to remove that lid.

Zain		 It's none other than your role to rescue Kudan from the darkness. Perhaps you had resolved yourself to that?

Kagura		 You might be right...

Kagura		 Until now, I have unknowingly been relying on Kudan.

Kagura		 I may have been relying on him to have power.

Kagura		 However...that's over now.

Kagura		 At that moment, I had thought clearly that I could not allow Kudan to get close to power.

Zain		 Heh. Power to distance him from power, huh? Make him feel the fear of power. That sounds like the kind of thing you'd come up with, Kagura.

Kagura		 That being said, since he's a prisoner to power right now, in order to save him, it may not be enough to fight his power.

Kagura		 If Kudan were to witness the great power of the Mikaboshi in that fateful land of Wadatsumi...

Kagura		 Like the heroes of old, I feel like he won't be able to return anymore.

Zain		 Yes. Besides, there are many others who are after the power of the Mikaboshi.

Zain		 If possible, we'd like to desperately avoid fighting with them, but...

Zain		 Word of such fights must not, under any circumstances, reach Kudan's ears.

Kagura		 Yes. I will distance Kudan from power.

Kagura		 Well then, let's hurry and go blow up that Mikaboshi! We can use Lord Zain's Excalibur and my...

Kagura		 Perfect timing! I'll make you watch the rebirth of my power until you get sick of it!
