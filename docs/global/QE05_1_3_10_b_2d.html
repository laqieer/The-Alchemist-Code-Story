A country in which many flourish by stealing from their neighbors, in which life is stolen by engineering the deaths of others.
In which culture and government are built upon the cruel reality of a dog-eat-dog society.
In which the weak have no means of protecting themselves from being swallowed up by the greedy and powerful.
