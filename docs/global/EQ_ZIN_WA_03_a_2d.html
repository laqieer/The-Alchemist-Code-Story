Sen I'm Sen. Are you a medicine man?

Jin		 Yes...I am.
Sen Please...! Save the village!

Arkil		 Sen, what happened? You have to give me the details for me to understand.
Sen Okay... My village...there are lots of people...with the same sickness as me!
Sen Our village has been making lots of swords from the past...!
Sen We are a swordsmith village with much history! My father is a great swordsman!
Sen At this rate...my village...its legacy...!

Jin		 I understand, Sen. Let's go to your village. Where is your village?
Sen It's further over the mountain...there's a valley...

Arkil		 H-hey! Sen!

Jin		 Don't worry, she's just sleeping.

Arkil		 I see. What a relief.

Jin		 With the intense fever cause by Demon's Blood, she climbed the mountain and came this far...she must have considerable strength.

Arkil		 Yes. All right! Let's go to Sen's village!

Jin		 Are you coming with me? I hear there are a lot of monsters up ahead of this mountain.

Jin		 It's dangerous.

Arkil		 It's a little late for that. Of course, I'm going with you.

Arkil		 Sen's village has one of this country's notable swordsmiths, right?

Arkil		 If I can buy myself a Wadatsumi blade, it'll be great for business!

Jin		 I see. I forgot that you were a carefree merchant.

Arkil		 Exactly.

Jin		 On my part, I would wish you would spread this medicine for Demon's Blood as soon as possible, but...

Arkil		 Besides, isn't there a proverb in this country?

Jin		 ...?

Arkil		 Like they say... "Wandering roads are the shortest path!"

Jin		 Do you mean, 'Good company is the shortest path'?

Jin		 Sigh... Fine, fine. Come with me then.

Jin		 I'll carry Sen. You protect yourself.

Arkil		 Got it! I won’t get in your way! But just...

Arkil		 Protect me if I'm in danger!

Jin		 ... ...

Arkil		 Phew... Mountain roads really are tough. They're a whole other game compared to walking through deserts.

Arkil		 Would you like to rest a little?

Jin		 We have a sick person here. Time is of the essence. Besides...

Jin		 Didn't you say you wouldn't cause problems for me?

Arkil		 Ri-right! I won't! All right, let's hurry! This is nothing!

Jin		 Don't push yourself! I thought this from when I first met you, but are you from a desert tribe?

Arkil		 Yeah. I was born in the Desert Zone.

Jin		 Just as I thought. Now I understand.

Arkil		 Hm? Understand what?

Jin		 Those from your land blow like the wind across the desert, untethered to anything, with dry cheer.

Jin		 You are the natural embodiment of your people.

Arkil		 The desert tribe originally became nomads in search of water.

Arkil		 Besides, with how hot and dry it gets in the desert, you wouldn't survive long if you took everything too seriously.

Arkil		 From the way you speak, you've been there before, haven't you?

Jin		 Yes. Just for a short period of time.

Jin		 It was a good place. They treated outsiders well with kindness.

Arkil		 I'm glad you like it!

Arkil		 Right! Didn't you like the food as well? Did you eat the sheep?

Jin		 Yes...but I didn't think the sheep tasted very good.

Jin		 I preferred the stewed beans.

Arkil		 That, huh? I really don't like stewed beans. What about our wheat noodles?

Jin		 Noodles? Yeah, they were good. I liked them too.

Arkil		 Right?! Ah, all this talk of food is making me hungry...

Arkil		 Even when traveling, you never forget the food of home.

Jin		 Heh. That's right. I just got back to Wadatsumi yesterday, and the first thing I ate was rice balls with pickled plum.

Jin		 It was so nostalgic I almost cried.

Arkil		 I see...

Jin		 Be careful. There's something there.

Arkil		 Is it a mountain monster? That didn't take long...

Jin		 ...It's coming!
