It was after the Lion Monarch's Charge that the Giant Taurus suddenly appeared.
This beast's destructive fury was such that it devastated the world, tearing open the still-smoldering wounds of war.
To combat this new threat, the Lord Commander used a device known as a Mecha Weapon. This granted him sufficient power to go head-to-head with the Giant Taurus and eventually defeat it.
Following this success, Judith, the Mecha Weapon's custodian, traveled around Babel on a campaign to wipe out any remaining members of the ruinous Taurus species.
What's more, the Holy Guard formed a special Taurus combat force, tasked with eliminating the creatures.
The combined efforts of these agents saw the majority of the species wiped from the face of Babel.
Yet some survived, and went on to evolve.
And now the Taurus threaten the continent of Babel once more.
