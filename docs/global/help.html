MENU_1 What is THE ALCHEMIST CODE?
MAIN_TITLE_1 What is THE ALCHEMIST CODE?
MAIN_TEXT_1 Mankind's Will is at the mere mercy of the Stones of Gods. History is woven as a continuation of the people's creation and loss. These are the memories of man crossing paths with The Stone at the source of all creation. This is a tactical RPG, built around the concept of a human drama following 7 protagonists from 7 different nations.

MAIN_TEXT_5 The sky-piercing tower which Nimul and Emmel, the founders of Alchemy, built using their power. It rises in the middle of the continent and can be seen from any of the land's kingdoms. It now stands as a symbol for the great foolish sin mankind has committed.
MENU_CATE_6 World Lore
MENU_6 Continent of Babel
MAIN_TITLE_6 Continent of Babel
MAIN_TEXT_6 The Continent of Babel is comprised of the far-reaching lands beneath the Tower of Babel, within which the Kingdom of Northern Pride holds a leading function. Located to the North is Slothstein, there are Lustburg and Greed Dike to the East, and Envylia, Old Wratharis and the Desert Zone to the West. Gluttony Foss and the Saga Region are spread out to the North of the Desert Zone.
MENU_CATE_7 World Lore

MAIN_TITLE_7 Seven Kingdoms of Babel
MAIN_TEXT_7 Long ago, there was a war between the Seven Kingdoms of the Babel Continent, in which Alchemy was used as a weapon. Because of its enormous destruction, and sensing its threat to human kind, the Nations of Babel formed the Alliance of the Seven Kingdoms, led by the suzerain state of Northern Pride. They branded Alchemy as the Forbidden Art.

MENU_CATE_15 Quests
MENU_15 About AP
MAIN_TITLE_15 About AP
MAIN_TEXT_15 AP is spent by taking on quests, etc. AP is replenished through time (1 point every 6m), and can also be replenished by spending Gems.
■Required Gems for AP replenishment
1st Time: 50 Gems
2nd-4th Time: 100 Gems
5th-6th Time: 200 Gems

■Replenished Amount
Each replenishment replenishes 120AP, up to a max of 1000AP.
*If your Max AP is under 120, replenishing AP will bring your AP above this limit.
Example: If your Max AP is 20, your AP will result in 20+120 (1 replenishment) = 140AP.
*If your AP is above your Max AP, your AP will not replenish over time until it falls below your Max AP limit.
Also, note that you cannot play any stages with AP cost higher than your Max AP.

MENU_CATE_95 VS Battle
MENU_95 What is VS Battle?
MAIN_TITLE_95 What is VS Battle?
MAIN_TEXT_95 Rules for VS Battles are as follows:
■Set up a party of up to 5 units.
■You can set up unit placement on the map by tapping Unit Placement on the setup screen.
■Normal attacks won't trigger Combo Attacks.
■If the battle hasn't been concluded before the turn limit, the side with the most remaining HP will win the battle. If remaining HP is the same, the battle will be a draw.
■Every player has 10s time to act from start of turn, +10s when moving, and +10s when selecting an action.
