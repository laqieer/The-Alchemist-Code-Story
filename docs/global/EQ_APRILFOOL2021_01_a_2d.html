Neville, Caesar, and Vlad—
Wratharis' Deadly Trio, the three pillars of Wratharis feared by warriors across all the kingdoms of Babel—
—find themselves engaged in a rather peculiar disagreement.
One week before the first of April...

Neville		 Hey, you guys, isn't it about time for our characters to be released?

Caesar		 Indeed. To think we've been passed over so many times already. Even those Blade Fox Corps demon slayers got in ahead of us, and they're from far later in the tale!

Vlad		 Could it be, perhaps, that we've been left on the cutting room floor, as it were?

Caesar		 Perish the thought! Our time is surely nigh.

Caesar		 After all, virtually the entire main story's cast has already seen release. We must be approaching the front of the line by now.

Vlad		 Yes. Yes, I suppose you're right. They've even added the Lizardman, for goodness' sake!

Caesar		 Quite right. And there's no way we're less desired by the masses than Lizardmen, so fear not! It's only a matter of time.

Caesar		 I'm certain we'll at least be featured in a Memento.

Vlad		 A Memento? But those are all based on characters who are still alive, aren't they?

Caesar		 Ah, that's right! I forget we were dead! Hahahaha. Regular Summons it is, then!

Vlad		 Summons, huh? Perhaps one of us could even be a guaranteed Summon. Imagine that!

Caesar		 That could happen. Or maybe instead, there could be a single-use Selector Ticket where the poor soul is forced to choose only one of us.

Vlad		 Oooh! So one of us guaranteed, but you won't know who until you draw? That'd sure be something!

Caesar		 The way TAC has been recently, it's quite possible. They've been on a roll these days, don't you think?

Vlad		 I'll say! I'm getting fired up just considering the possibilities!

Neville		 Of course, all of this is only if we get added.

Caesar		 It'll happen! Mark my words! I swear, Neville, all you do is worry. It's simply foolish to think that they wouldn't add us.

Neville		 I'd like to believe that, truly. But I can't help feeling anxious. What if...everyone's forgotten about us?

Caesar		 Hah! Inconceivable!

Neville		 But how can you say that? We've barely had any screen time.

Caesar		 The time we have been given most assuredly left a lasting impression, though.

Neville		 What makes you so confident about that?

Caesar		 Well, consider this: every time a user starts up the game with a less than optimal signal, they're presented with lore-rich backstory, no?

Neville		 Game? Is this a game to you?! Ah, but I suppose it's too late to pull back the fourth wall now. At any rate, you are correct: there is much backstory to be had. What of it?

Caesar		 When that backstory is displayed, there is sometimes a picture of our concept art accompanying it, no?

Neville		 That's true, there is.

Caesar		 ...

Neville		 ......

Caesar		 Well, there you have it!

Neville		 That's it?! Your confidence comes from that alone?!

Vlad		 Hmm, he does have a point. Having us feature there is a rather big deal.

Neville		 It's really not, though.

Caesar		 How can you say that? If you wish to play this game, you have to go through us first!

Neville		 I doubt most people are even looking at their screens when that picture displays, though.

Caesar		 Nonetheless, if you can secure a position like that right from the get-go, I'd say you've already won the battle! And the war can't be far behind.

Vlad		 Like owning land in Roppongi since before it became prime real estate?

Neville		 Again with that fantasy world of yours! There's no such place.

Caesar		 Anyway, what I'm saying here is that there's simply no way the powers that be have forgotten about us.

Neville		 Perhaps not, but they may very well be looking down on us.

Vlad		 What do you mean?

Neville		 Let's say, for argument's sake, that Caesar is correct, and we have not been forgotten. If that's the case, would this late release not indicate that we're being looked down upon?

Caesar		 If we are, then I dare say it's your fault, Neville.

Neville		 What? Why me?!

Caesar		 Because of the tutorial.

Neville		 The tutorial?

Caesar		 In the tutorial, you're introduced as having been specially schooled by Vlad.

Neville		 Yes, that's...correct. But what of it?

Caesar		 This suggests to players that you are a top-tier fighter—virtually unbeatable!

Neville		 Stop, stop! You flatter me.

Caesar		 Oh, I assure you, I do not. For though you are built up so much, you are then immediately bested by a couple snot-nosed kids.

Neville		 Pardon?!

Vlad		 Ah, that's very true! I couldn't believe it myself; all that training amounted to nothing in the end!

Neville		 That's par for the course in this sort of story! I was a tutorial boss! That's not going to shape the players' perception of us!

Caesar		 I disagree. If we are looked down upon, I can think of no better explanation as to why. Think back on it good and hard, and I'm sure you'll agree.
