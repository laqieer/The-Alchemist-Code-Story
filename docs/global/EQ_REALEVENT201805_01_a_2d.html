On a certain day in 2018...
Let's take a peek at the operations team preparing for the combined event, "Phantom of the Kill & The Alchemist Code Summit".
They are in the middle of designing a second challenge for your entertainment.

TAC Producer		 Here we are in Kobe!

TAC Producer		 It seems like we went from the planning stage to the big day in the blink of an eye! Is everything ready to go, Designer?

TAC Designer		 Good question. The stage events and merchandise are all set to go. Should we review the course of events for the quest?

TAC Producer		 Sounds good. Artist should be here soon, too, so we can go over everything together!

TAC Artist		 Designer, I bought some treats in Kyoto to share with everyone! They make so many flavors, it was hard to choose.

TAC Artist		 Ah! Producer?! I'm so sorry I'm late! Did something happen with the event?

TAC Producer		 No, no. Everything is fine. We were just about to go over the details of the quest.

TAC Producer		 But I must say, you're really excited about these treats. Watching you enjoy them so much makes me happy.

TAC Designer		 Oh, I know! Why don't we work that into the quest, too?

TAC Producer		 It might not get a big laugh, but I can just see it now! The curtain rises, and Artist says, "I brought treats!" Thus, the enemy is charmed! I like it!

TAC Artist		 Oh, so there's going to be stuff like that in the quest, too, huh? Charmed...is kinda an embarrassing way of putting it, though...

TAC Designer		 It's fine! Don't worry about it! It'll be great! It's just another one of those quests where you're encouraged to protect Artist.

TAC Designer		 We've already decided that a charmed enemy will be attacking you, Artist. I'm just glad we finally found a reason for them to get charmed in the first place.

TAC Artist		 A quest where everyone protects me... Even though I knew it would be that kind of thing, I still feel like I need to apologize to everyone.

TAC Designer		 Well, that's the whole plot device of this quest.

TAC Producer		 If you defeat Artist, then Designer will be upset and say something like, "How could you?!" and probably get a massive power up!

TAC Designer		 Yeah, and if Artist is all right, she'll just come back as if nothing happened.

TAC Producer		 But Designer will just show up again. That's what happened in the last one! That's boring. Can't we have something more, well, sensational?

TAC Artist		 Sensational? If we think about it like a content creator would, then... How about, "Vampire and Zombie"?

TAC Designer		 Okay. So what you're saying is that we'll come out at the same time. Something like... "The Vampire can't be defeated while the Zombie is protecting it."

TAC Producer		 Those would be our PR ambassadors? They don't seem fitting with TAC.

TAC Artist		 A vampire, a zombie, and Designer? That's a bit of a heavy responsibility, isn't it?

TAC Designer		 Then, when you defeat the three of them, that's when Producer will appear, right? Some smaller enemies were supposed to pop up alongside him, but maybe we could change that, too.

TAC Producer		 Maybe a random TAC celebrity could appear. They're all really busy, so you never know who's going to show up.

TAC Producer		 Don't forget about Director Ratty! He could give me a buff that makes me super strong, or something cool like that!

TAC Artist		 I guess he could cast a buff that can't be dispelled.

TAC Producer		 Yeah, like a final boss!

TAC Producer		 So, if they can defeat Director Ratty before the balance adjustment is complete, I'll be weakened.

TAC Producer		 It would be cool if my business cards contained Mementos and Vision Abilities so people with my cards could use them in battles.

TAC Producer		 You two could cheer on the player and get them all fired up when they use the Vision Abilities!

TAC Designer		 All right. Got it.

TAC Producer		 All right, I'm counting on you! If they can defeat me in the quest, they'll get a very exciting reward.

TAC Artist		 You can get a Lion Set and Enchantment Coins, right?

TAC Artist		 Players will still be able to collect coins for the Enchanted Jobs for a while, too! I'm really excited about it, myself!

TAC Producer		 Right! I wouldn't let them make the rewards smaller than they were in the last one!

TAC Designer		 (Is it okay to give out so much? I'll have to check.)

TAC Designer		 Anyway, I guess that's about it for this quest, right?

TAC Producer		 Um... Well, since we're going to Kobe and all... You know what they say! When in Rome, do as the Romans do!

TAC Artist		 When in Rome...?

TAC Designer		 Wh-what do you mean?

TAC Producer		 Let's go Kansai style!

TAC Designer		 That's a great idea! Atmosphere is important, after all. We should change how we talk, and our outfits and stuff.

TAC Artist		 I'll have to check the script, but I bet the costumes will be good.

TAC Artist		 Something like this, maybe? Don't I look elegant?

TAC Producer		 Looks great! We should start looking for something to wear, too!

TAC Designer		 Hm... The size is right, but aren't they a bit too much?

TAC Artist		 Not at all! You look all wild and cool!

TAC Designer		 If you say so... Wait, Producer hasn't come back yet?

TAC Producer		 So this is... I don't think I'm allowed to say their name...but doesn't it look like a certain someone's clothes?

TAC Designer		 Whoa! That's even flashier than I am!

TAC Artist		 You can tell whose it is just by looking at it! All that red and white... It's really impressive!

TAC Producer		 Heh, even if it's just a rehash of the last live event's outfit, it still feels brand new!

TAC Producer		 We're gonna make sure that this second TAC Live Event has a unique sense of drama, and an interesting strategy worthy of our audience!

TAC Designer		 Got it!

TAC Artist		 I'll do my best this time around, too!

TAC Producer		 That's the spirit! Let's do this!

TAC Designer		 I'm going to go and let them know about the changes to the quest. There's still time... I hope.

TAC Artist		 Okay. If they need anything from us, let us know! We'll do our best!

TAC Designer		 We went to a lot of trouble to make this villain fight. I won't be satisfied unless it's at least a little bit more interesting than last time.

TAC Designer		 The quest we just hashed out definitely sounds like it'll be more interesting. Well, Artist, good luck with the event!

TAC Artist		 Okay, you too! I'll give it everything I've got!

TAC Artist		 And there you have it! This will be the operations team's second official TAC challenge!

TAC Artist		 We've done our best to scatter hints throughout the story this time around, too!

TAC Artist		 Battles even fiercer than the previous challenge await.

TAC Artist		 Alchemists! Accept our challenge, and follow the trail of clues we have left for you to beat The Alchemist Code's operations team at their own game!

TAC Artist		 We'll be there to support you as best we can!

TAC Artist		 Oh, and we really tried to make it Kansai style, too, but please forgive us if we failed!
