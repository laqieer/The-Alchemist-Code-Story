Waginau		 Wh-where...? What have I...?
Zafeiris Glad to see you awake, Seeker.

Zafeiris Do you not remember? As the light approached, you used all the power you had to save them both.

Waginau		 I did? I have no memory of this. And on that note, where are we?
Zafeiris So your actions were done unconsciously? I did not expect the assimilation to have already progressed so far.

Waginau		 I-I must return to Nimul and the others!
Zafeiris That is not possible at this moment.

Waginau		 What? Why not?!

Zafeiris This is the Shell of the World—the place where the Pursuer, Nimul's, eyes were opened to the Truth of Alchemy. This is where it all began.

Waginau		 Here?
Zafeiris This is an opportunity that should not have been. It is something we never had a chance to even dream of before.

Waginau		 I don't care what sort of opportunity it is! How do I get back to where I was?! I must protect Nimul, or else—
Zafeiris Is that your sense of responsibility as a warrior of Sephira, and his duty-bound attendant, talking? Or do you speak these words as his friend?

Waginau		 Zafeiris?
Zafeiris Regardless of which it may be, it is simply not possible for you to return. There is something you must do first.

Waginau		 What do you mean? What do I have to do?
Zafeiris The Pursuer, Nimul, has reached heights comparable to our god.
Zafeiris The Stairs of Creation...
Zafeiris He's taken the first step into the beginnings of a new world. He's begun the Ceremony of Creation.

Waginau		 Wait, the Ceremony of...? Nimul had rejected that ceremony, saying that another path must be taken.
Zafeiris The Infinite Serpent must have wrapped themselves around his throat, influencing his mind.
Zafeiris You can still stop him. There is still time. That is, of course, if you are willing to make the appropriate sacrifice.

Waginau		 Is it...truly possible?
Zafeiris Are you not going to ask what the appropriate sacrifice is?

Waginau		 It's rather obvious what's being asked of me.

Waginau		 The ceremony requires my life, does it not?
Zafeiris To stop Nimul, you must earn the right to stand shoulder to shoulder, upon the same stage as he.
Zafeiris This place gives you that opportunity.

Waginau		 All right. So what must I do, exactly?
Zafeiris You must consume my memories.
Zafeiris If there is something you lack, then all you need to do is take it from another.
Zafeiris If you cannot reach a certain height through diligent training, you must simply evolve yourself into a being who can.
Zafeiris That is the nature of Nimul's creation: Alchemy.
Zafeiris Change through usurpation... Rebirth through plundering... A forceful impelling of the world, in defiant rejection of stagnation.
Zafeiris If you are willing to act in accordance with these doctrines, there is yet a path left for you to take.

Waginau		 You mean...I am to wield Alchemy?
Zafeiris With that sword in your hand, I entreat you to run me through.

Waginau		 You wish to be pierced by this blade, which holds the capacity to slay dragons? I do recall Yna mentioning the blade being engraved with Alchemy...

Waginau		 But... What would become of you if I did?
Zafeiris Do not worry about me. I am merely a vestige, of sorts, destined to vanish without a trace.

Waginau		 Th-This is the Cursed Beasts'...!
Zafeiris This is my true nature—the mark of our sin.
Zafeiris Now... Do it.
