Basheeny		 You can only be here for one reason. You've come to erase me, haven't you?

Irene		 Corpse's duty is twofold: to deliver a warning to those who threaten to stumble on the truth, and to eliminate those who do come too close.

Irene		 Why would you come here, knowing the price you would pay?

Basheeny		 In order to preserve the order of this world.

Irene		 That is not your burden to bear.

Irene		 The order of this world is ours to protect. The Lord Commander must serve as a guiding light...but in your weakness, the light you cast is frail.

Irene		 Poor, pallid Lord Commander, wavering in the darkness... I will gauge the threat you pose by my own hand.

Basheeny		 How can you stay with Corpse, knowing Waginau's true goals?

Irene		 As long as he does not threaten the world's order, he is no concern of mine.

Basheeny		 Well, if you think I'll go quietly despite that, you've got another thing coming!

Basheeny		 Nngh...!

Irene		 Your doubts and your hastiness still dull your blade.

Irene		 You have failed to heed my lessons.

Basheeny		 Why do you still follow Waginau, with this kind of strength at your command?

Irene		 When new and disruptive powers arise, those who would use them for evil ends soon follow. Corpse secures such powers and keeps them from the masses.

Irene		 Powers such as the Mecha Weapon that stood against the Giant Taurus.

Basheeny		 Then...it was you!

Basheeny		 You were the one who killed Judith's father!

Irene		 I did not wield the blade myself.

Irene		 But you should understand better than anyone the nature of our work.

Irene		 Whenever a destabilizing power emerges, Corpse intervenes to secure or eliminate it before it can plunge the world into chaos.

Irene		 Thus is order preserved, and we will stop at nothing to maintain it.

Irene		 All the more so when some unwitting fool stumbles across the secret of the Cursed Beasts.

Basheeny		 I cannot deny that great power can bring catastrophe if it falls into the wrong hands...but not everyone is so callous.

Basheeny		 There are those who seek power for the good of all. To protect the world, not to plunge it into chaos.

Basheeny		 I have known such people myself. I once walked by their side.

Basheeny		 My resolve is their legacy...and so I cannot allow anyone to stand in my way.

Irene		 Resolve serves no end but to undermine this world's order.

Basheeny		 Once upon a time, I was just another Corpse recruit under your command. But now...

Basheeny		 Now I am the Lord Commander, the living embodiment of absolute justice!
