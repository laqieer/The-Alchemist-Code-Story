A Road in Lustburg

Albea		 The snow is already piling up.

Albea		 (Deneb...)

Albea		 (This fortuneteller is known for being incredibly precise. She divined my brother's whereabouts for me once.)

Albea		 (She might be able to tell me more; this time in more detail...)

Albea		 (But...)

Albea		 (I can't seem to bring myself to even knock on her door.)

Sophia		 The snow is piling up, you know?

Albea		 Huh?

Sophia		 Goodness! What pretty hair you have!

Albea		 Ah, um, I'm s-sorry...

Sophia		 Are you here to see Deneb?

Albea		 ...

Sophia		 I am sorry, but Deneb is going to be on holiday for a while.

Sophia		 Her store is closed, in fact, although I am tending it today in her absence.

Albea		 ...

Sophia		 Would you like some tea?

Albea		 Deneb is all right, isn't she?

Sophia		 She is. I am afraid that I can't tell you where she is, however.

Albea		 Th-that's...fine.

Sophia		 Oh? What is this? A telescope?

Albea		 Could you...give this to Deneb for me?

Albea		 Tell her I said thank you, and that...

Albea		 I'm finally getting close to an answer, and will do my best to find it.

Sophia		 You...

Albea		 I'd better get going.

Sophia		 Wait! Hold on a moment!

Albea		 Wh-what is it?

Sophia		 Is your name...Albea?

Albea		 Ah...

Albea		 Achoo!

Sophia		 You've been out in the cold for a while, haven't you? Here. Have some nice, warm tea.

Albea		 Th-thank you.

Sophia		 I haven't introduced myself yet, have I? My name is Sophia.

Albea		 ...

Sophia		 Is something the matter? Is something on my...?

Albea		 It's just... You keep playing with your hair, as if you're...trying to cover your ears, or something.

Sophia		 Oh, that! It's just a silly habit of mine.

Albea		 ...

Sophia		 P-please excuse me.

Albea		 N-no, I'm sorry! I worry about my hair, too. I shouldn't have—!

Sophia		 I've heard about you from Deneb. She said that someday, a pink light would come to look anew upon the stars.

Albea		 So she was expecting me.

Sophia		 You're exactly as she described. Look at that large contraption on your back! You really like machines, don't you?

Albea		 Oh, this? This is something my father left me. We made it together, actually. I prefer miniature ones myself, though.

Sophia		 It must be awfully important to you. Do you carry it with you everywhere?

Albea		 It requires a huge amount of Alchemia to function, so I can't power it on my own.

Albea		 There's just someone who I...really want to show it to, whether it's working or not.

Sophia		 Is that where you're headed?

Albea		 I'm looking for my older brother.

Sophia		 Ah, yes. Deneb told me that you asked her to divine his location.

Sophia		 What brought you back here, then?

Albea		 Well, it's just... I'm not sure anymore. I feel like I'm getting closer to my brother, but apparently that's dangerous.

Albea		 I've come this far, and if it was just me, I wouldn't mind getting involved in something risky.

Albea		 It's when I think about dragging someone else into harm's way that I get scared. I didn't know I could feel so...afraid.

Sophia		 Hmm...

Albea		 Thank you for the tea.

Sophia		 Are you leaving already?

Albea		 I don't want to trouble you.

Sophia		 ...

Sophia		 Albea. Look at me.

Albea		 H-huh?

Albea		 Th-those are...!

Albea		 Elf ears!

Sophia		 You needn't worry about putting me in harm's way.

Albea		 Wh-what do you mean?

Sophia		 Elves have very good hearing, you see. Not only that, but we are a people who have been on the verge of being oppressed and used for selfish gain many times over. If nothing else, I am used to danger.

Albea		 O-oppressed...?

Sophia		 I can help you.

Albea		 B-but...

Sophia		 You remind me of someone, you know.

Sophia		 I have a friend who is waiting for the return of their younger sister.

Sophia		 They always smile fondly when they speak of her.

Sophia		 Yet I am convinced that they are not truly happy in her absence.

Albea		 I...!

Sophia		 Please. Allow me to lend you my strength.

Albea		 Wh-why is there ice in here—and it's...floating?!

Sophia		 Where is the star whose shadow falls upon the ice? Where is the soul which drifts beneath the floe? With an eye above and an eye below, I will divine their radiant glow!

Sophia		 Albea, do you have anything of your brother's? Something that resonates with him?

Albea		 Something...that resonates with my brother?

Albea		 Ah! I do! H-here!

Sophia		 Is this...a military uniform from the army of Wratharis?

Albea		 My mother gave it to me a long time ago.

Albea		 She made it for me when I was little, for me to grow into. It's based on my brother's uniform.

Albea		 It's the only real memory I have of my brother.

Sophia		 May I borrow it?

Albea		 P-please take good care of it.

Sophia		 But of course.

Albea		 What are you going to do with it?

Sophia		 Project the star steeped in memories upon the ice to reveal the trail.

Sophia		 Also...

Albea		 Y-yes?

Sophia		 We will be needing a spell to make sure that everything goes well!

Albea		 J-just make sure you give it back!

Sophia		 Ohohoh! Do you have so little faith in me?

Albea		 If I didn't have faith in you, I wouldn't have handed it to you in the first place!

Sophia		 Oh, dear.

Albea		 ...

Albea		 Please!

Sophia		 Hm...

Sophia		 Your brother's star has sunk to the depths of the river of rage, leaving its light scrambled upon the surface.

Albea		 The river of rage? Does that mean...Wratharis?

Sophia		 Upriver lies the source of the star stream itself. It lies still, with open maw.

Albea		 The source of the...star stream?

Sophia		 The ancient capital, tied to the forbidden art.

Albea		 The forbidden art? Alchemy? The ancient...?

Albea		 Wait! Does this mean that there are ruins in Wratharis like the ones in Slothstein?

Sophia		 Eventually, your brother's star will be drawn spiraling into the center and engulfed...

Sophia		 Taken to the place of the sealed.

Sophia		 Ah!

Albea		 I knew it!

Sophia		 Who are these—?!

Albea		 They're annoying, that's what they are!

Sophia		 I see! They're a danger, then, I take it.

Albea		 You've got that right!
