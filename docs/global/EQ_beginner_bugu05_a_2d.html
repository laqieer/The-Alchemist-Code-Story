Eizan		 Do you understand so far? By the way, the current technology on the continent only allows you to evolve until 5★.

Eizan		 All right, from here I'm going to give a little more advanced lessons that are important for becoming stronger.

Eizan		 According to a unit's Limit Break Count, the number of pieces of gear that are equippable increases.

Eizan		 At first, all units can equip one piece of gear. Limit Breaking 10 times allows two, and 25 times allows three pieces of gear to be equipped.

Eizan		 In other words, a unit can equip a maximum of three pieces of gear.

Eizan		 Also, there are three different types of gear. Weapons, Armor, and Accessories.

Eizan		 I mentioned this at the beginning, but the gear chooses the user.

Eizan		 Some gear items are special and can only be equipped by specific Jobs.

Eizan		 There's no point in someone who fights with a sword carrying a gun, right? In other words, a sword-fighting Job should carry a sword.

Eizan		 Also, I said you can use a maximum of three pieces of gear, but you can only equip one Weapon and one Armor each.

Eizan		 You can equip up to three accessories. However, you can't equip gear with the same name.

Eizan		 No making your repertoire Doll, Doll, and Doll. You can't do that and it's just too cute.

Eizan		 When two pieces of gear have the same effect, only the stronger one will have an effect, so no duplicate effects can be used. Use caution.

Eizan		 For example: if an HP +100 Armor and HP +50 Weapon are equipped, the HP +100 effect will be prioritized.

Eizan		 Well then, from here I'll teach you the about the strength of gear. In other words, your gear's true power.

Eizan		 Depending on the gear you use, some have special powers. Those are the gear with souls.

Eizan		 If you equip these, you can use their special skills. These can range from Magic to Recover, etc.

Eizan		 For example: even if your Job doesn't have a Recovery Skill, you will be able to use that skill when using certain gear.

Eizan		 This may allow you to drastically change and improve. Although, on the flip end, it's not your regular skill, so it can sometimes consume more Jewels.

Eizan		 Amongst those, there is gear specified for certain units. If a unit uses one of these, it will serve them well in battle.

Eizan		 Such gear is powerful. Depending on the user, it can allow a powerful Gear Ability to be used.

Eizan		 There is a noticeable difference in strength between units that can and cannot use Gear Abilities. You should try testing it out in the Arena.

Eizan		 Phew...I'm not really this talkative usually, so I'm a bit tired now... Well then, let's try to evolve to 5★.

Eizan		 In order to evolve to 5★, gear needs to be at Lv 25. Also, it requires 100 Moon Star Armor Shards.
