001 Do you understand so far? By the way, the current technology on the continent only allows you to evolve until 5★.
002 All right, from here I'm going to give a little more advanced lessons that are important for becoming stronger.
003 According to a unit's Limit Break Count, the number of pieces of gear that are equippable increases.
004 At first, all units can equip one piece of gear. Limit Breaking 10 times allows two, and 25 times allows three pieces of gear to be equipped.
005 In other words, a unit can equip a maximum of three pieces of gear.
006 Also, there are three different types of gear. Weapons, Armor, and Accessories.
007 I mentioned this at the beginning, but the gear chooses the user.
008 Some gear items are special and can only be equipped by specific Jobs.
009 There's no point in someone who fights with a sword carrying a gun, right? In other words, a sword-fighting Job should carry a sword.
010 Also, I said you can use a maximum of three pieces of gear, but you can only equip one Weapon and one Armor each.
011 You can equip up to three accessories. However, you can't equip gear with the same name.
012 No making your repertoire Doll, Doll, and Doll. You can't do that and it's just too cute.
013 When two pieces of gear have the same effect, only the stronger one will have an effect, so no duplicate effects can be used. Use caution.
014 For example: if an HP +100 Armor and HP +50 Weapon are equipped, the HP +100 effect will be prioritized.
015 Well then, from here I'll teach you the about the strength of gear. In other words, your gear's true power.
016 Depending on the gear you use, some have special powers. Those are the gear with souls.
017 If you equip these, you can use their special skills. These can range from Magic to Recover, etc.
018 For example: even if your Job doesn't have a Recovery Skill, you will be able to use that skill when using certain gear.
019 This may allow you to drastically change and improve. Although, on the flip end, it's not your regular skill, so it can sometimes consume more Jewels.
020 Amongst those, there is gear specified for certain units. If a unit uses one of these, it will serve them well in battle.
021 Such gear is powerful. Depending on the user, it can allow a powerful Gear Ability to be used.
022 There is a noticeable difference in strength between units that can and cannot use Gear Abilities. You should try testing it out in the Arena.
