Before the two had realized it, food became the bridge that connected humans and demons.
They both realized, even if subconsciously, that their futures were intertwined.
And they set about changing the minds of their respective races--in alignment with their cause.
The human expressed his resolve to his queen.
It would have been simple enough to appeal to her emotions, but he did not.
"A path exists in which we don't need to fight demons."
"If we provide food that demons can eat, they won't need to attack humans anymore. And I've developed such food."
The queen stated, "I need to see it with my own eyes before I believe you."
So the human spoke again, choosing his words carefully.
"I'll show you proof."
"If I'm lying, you can have my head."
The demon declared her resolve to her brother, in addition to recounting everything that had taken place until that point.
Facts alone weren't enough to convince him, so she appealed to his emotions.
"I've discovered food that I can eat."
"You can finally be relieved of the burden you've carried."
"And I can live the 'normal' life I've been hoping for."
The demon chief posed a question:
"Of what food do you speak, exactly?"
The demon steeled herself and choose her words carefully:
"We can survive without devouring humans--no one need starve. We can finally live in peace."
"And what if peace never comes to pass?"
"Then I will gladly offer my head."
"Is there some form of proof you can show me?"
The brother asked his sister:
"Why should demons join forces with humans when they show us nothing but contempt?"
The demon asserted to her brother.
"There are some humans who show us compassion."
Her brother spoke deliberately, making the demon second-guess herself.
"Devouring humans is what demons do."
The demon chose her words carefully, in the hopes of reaching her brother.
"If so, then I don't need to be a demon. I have faith in a better future."
And so, for the first time, humans and demons…
Communicated without violence.
