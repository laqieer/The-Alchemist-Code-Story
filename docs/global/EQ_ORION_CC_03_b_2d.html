A mix of emotions flooded through me on this first journey of mine to another land, but the strongest among them at this moment was the sense that something just...wasn't quite right.
Although Langhao had achieved her goal and gotten her hands on Mammon, nothing—not her words, nor her actions, nor her mannerisms—seemed in keeping with the Langhao I'd come to know.
Regardless, I had been bested, as far as I knew. So after a time, upon our return to Greed Dike...
We were called before the current emperor and informed that Langhao had officially been chosen as the successor, in my place.
Evidently, the arranged marriage had been agreed upon, too. And so, the first empress in the history of Greed Dike came to be, with ties to Wratharis in tow.
It should have been a happy occasion for her, but each time I shot her a sidewards glance, she always looked very unsure of herself.
I decided to follow through with this observation, and see where the thread may lead.
