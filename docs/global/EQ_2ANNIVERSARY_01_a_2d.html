——On a certain day in 2018 (FgG Studios)
Let's take a peek at the operations team preparing The Alchemist Code's first ever live event...
There is a sense of anticipation as the team discusses what the event should include to make sure the fans have the ultimate experience.

TAC Artist		 This event is really coming together! We're well on our way to finishing our preparations for the exhibitions, cafes, and goods...

TAC Designer		 Indeed. It's amazing how far we've come! Everyone's worked so hard. I'm sure that the players will appreciate what we've done with this event.

TAC Producer		 ——Drama! Strategy!
TAC Designer, TAC Artist P-Producer?

TAC Producer		 I've been looking for you two. This event plan is lacking the punch to fully immerse people into the world of The Alchemist's Code!

TAC Designer		 It is? I feel like we've already prepared everything we possibly can, though.

TAC Producer		 What draws people to The Alchemist Code is the drama—and the strategy!

TAC Producer		 The goods, the cafe, and the exhibition will help people better visualize the world we've created, but where are the aspects that feature the strategy element of the game?

TAC Artist		 I see! That's what you meant by "lacking".

TAC Producer		 Exactly. That said...since this is our first live event, I have absolutely no idea what we could do to highlight the strategy side of the game.

TAC Producer		 Hm... Our Phantom of the Kill game incorporates a lot of opportunities to interact directly with the players, so it's easier to come up with ideas, but...

TAC Producer		 We mostly interact with players of The Alchemist's Code through the Nico Nico Live video streaming site, so something like that is the only thing I can really think of.

TAC Designer		 Yeah... What else might we do to feature the strategy elements other than releasing high-difficulty quests?

TAC Producer		 ——Aha! I've got it, Designer!

TAC Producer		 Since we're not sure what the players would enjoy, we ought to use Nico Nico Live to tackle the players head-on!

TAC Artist		 What?!

TAC Designer		 That's... I mean, what...?

TAC Producer		 Designer, you'll be participating, of course.

TAC Designer		 H-huh?

TAC Designer		 ——I see... So you meant that we should stream a Multiplay quest on Nico Nico Live in which we are the enemies that need to be defeated.

TAC Artist		 Oh, man... That's a relief. I get it now.

TAC Producer		 Yes. I think this is the perfect way to solve our crisis! Use Nico Nico Live as a framework to battle directly with the fans.

TAC Producer		 "The true boss is...Producer?!" is the kind of thing we're going for, basically.

TAC Producer		 I'll be the ultimate boss challenge! Then in exchange for getting soundly beat by my skills, the players will have a chance to get some fantastic rewards!

TAC Artist		 T-that's quite a plan!

TAC Producer		 We still need some element of humor in all this... Like some kind of Alchemist Code celebrity appearance.

TAC Designer		 Ah... What if we introduce a kind of celebrity summoning ability for your character?

TAC Artist		 I-I don't know...

TAC Producer		 Hm, I don't know how willing the players will be to attack someone like Artist. Maybe we could have her shield us instead?

TAC Designer		 I don’t think the ugly tension of "Will the players attack Artist or not?" is the kind of atmosphere we want to promote at our first event.

TAC Producer		 But not only would she be our shield, she would also play a role that supports the players!

TAC Artist		 I-I see...?

TAC Producer		 We will unleash a powerful Strike when cornered, but players will be able to borrow Artist's ability to save themselves!

TAC Producer		 What do you think? Isn't that a nice support role for Artist? I think the players will enjoy that.

TAC Artist		 I-I would be honored to take on such a role!

TAC Producer		 Wonderful! I know we can rely on you to give the players the best TAC experience they could hope for!

TAC Producer		 Oh, and Designer ought to take advantage of that function which allows bosses to change form during battle! I mean, we developed it, we might as well use it.

TAC Producer		 And you...can do...that.

TAC Designer		 ..."That"?

TAC Producer		 You know. That thing you do whenever we're on Nico Nico Live. That.

TAC Designer		 What?! I've told you before, that was a misunderstanding!

TAC Artist		 I-indeed! His looks have no bearing on the kind of wonderful partner he is!

TAC Designer		 Artist... What... What are you...?

TAC Producer		 Umm... Anyway, what about changing your unit's attack type mid-battle? Like, from a magic type to a physical type!

TAC Designer		 I guess that could work...

TAC Producer		 Just me left, then... As the Nico Nico Live streaming progresses, I shall do my utmost to become the most powerful villain!

TAC Producer		 I'll make generous use of Cadanova's ability, Dragon Shift, to make the battle especially difficult for the players!

TAC Producer		 And we'll set it up so players have to borrow Artist's strength to defend themselves...

TAC Producer		 And then, once the players have defeated me...

TAC Artist		 You can reveal your special final combined form "Super Producer" that can give out lots of fantastic prizes!

TAC Producer		 Heh... Perfect! We have to go all out for our first live event!

TAC Producer		 No holds barred! Let's make the players work for their win against us!

TAC Designer		 Understood.

TAC Artist		 I'll do my very best!

TAC Producer		 Fantastic! I have faith that the two of you can make this Multiplay quest a wonderfully challenging experience for everyone!

TAC Designer		 So... No pressure.

TAC Artist		 D-don't lose heart, Designer! Let's all do our best to make this into an experience people can't forget! I'll do everything I can as well. You're not alone in this!

TAC Designer		 Right...

TAC Designer		 Right. It's not often that the chance comes around to play the villain. I'll do my best to make it a battle the players really have to fight through!

TAC Designer		 I can already tell that this is going to be quite the journey...but I think the end result will be really good. This is inspiring!

TAC Artist		 I completely understand! When the time comes, I'll come at the players as their worst enemy!

TAC Designer		 Wait! Hold on! You're there to help the players for this event! So, on that note... I've got a bunch of work ahead of me. I'm going to go get started on that.

TAC Artist		 ——So, in conclusion, this is the operations team's official challenge!

TAC Artist		 We've done our best to scatter hints throughout this little story! I hope they help!

TAC Artist		 Alchemists, accept our challenge and follow the trail of clues we have left for you to beat The Alchemist Code's operations team at their own game!

TAC Artist		 I'll be there to support you as best I can!

TAC Artist		 *This dramatized scenario is based on real events.
